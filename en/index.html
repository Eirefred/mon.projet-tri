Our Promise: Adaptation

Preparing for a triathlon can seem complex or daunting, and not everyone knows where to start. While excellent traditional clubs exist, their scheduling or geographical constraints don't work for everyone.

We empower those who train alone—by choice or by isolation—to access coach-mentors who guide every step of their progress. Through flexible, connected tracking, we break down geographical barriers to offer attentive support, regardless of your location in Quebec.

As a registered non-profit, we prioritize human relationships and direct support. Our non-profit structure allows us to offer high-level expertise at the most affordable rates possible, reinvesting our resources exclusively into the quality of your guidance. Our mission is to share our knowledge to save you time and ensure the full realization of your athletic project.

Program: First Step

"One day, I'm going to do one..."

This program is the heart of our mission:
getting you to cross your very first finish line.

  • Assignment of a dedicated mentor based on your goals.
  • Complete guidance for purchasing your first gear.
  • 12-week training plan adapted to your current level.
  • Plan adjustments to fit your lifestyle.
  • Explanations of how a triathlon unfolds.
  • Training on race rules and regulations.
  • Moral and psychological support for your first starting line.
Price: $399 all-included for 3 months($133/month)
Training plan + 5 mentorship sessions (40 min each)
